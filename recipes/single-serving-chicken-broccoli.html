Single Serving Chicken with Broccoli and Rice

Ingredients

  • Chicken Tenderloins
  • Broccoli Crown
  • 1 Cup Jasmine Rice
  • Your favorite asian/bbq sauce

Steps

  1. Add 1 cup of Jasmine Rice to rice cooker
  2. Place chicken in pan on stove or grill and cook over high heat until fully cooked
  3. Cut up broccoli crown into smaller pieces and place in water in a pan on the stove
  4. Cook broccoli over high heat until it is soft enough for a fork to slide in
  5. Plate rice from the cooker next to chicken and a scoop of broccoli
  6. Add your favorite sauce for some extra yummy flavor
