Tacos Recipe

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning Packet
  • 2/3 Cup Water
  • Taco Shells
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatos
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Taco Sauce

Steps

  1. Place ground beef in pan on stove over medium heat and cook until browned
  2. Drain grease from pan
  3. Add 2/3 cup of water and the taco seasoning packet to the ground beef in pan
  4. Continue to cook over low heat, covered, for a few more minutes
  5. Grab a taco shell on plate and add meat
  6. Dress taco with lettuce, tomato, and cheese as you like
  7. Add taco sauce and enjoy!
