Spaghetti Recipe

Ingedients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Put water in a pan to boil on the stove
  2. Once water is boiling, add spaghetti noodles and salt to taste
  3. While the noodles are cooking place the tomato sauce in a seperate pan over low heat
  4. Cook noodles approximately 8-12 minutes until al dente
  5. Serve the noodles on a plate and add as much sauce as you like
